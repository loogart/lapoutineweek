What is La Poutine Week?

Every year, from the 1st to 7th of February, restaurants from different cities across Canada make special poutines just for La Poutine Week. Poutine-lovers all over get a chance to discover these delicious creations and vote for their favorite ones on LaPoutineWeek.com
