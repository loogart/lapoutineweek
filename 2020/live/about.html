Eat Poutine and help others.A Great Cause.

For 30 years now, Anorexie et Boulimie Québec (ANEB) has been around to offer hope and support to people suffering from eating disorders across Quebec. This section will allow you to understand the organisation’s history and how ANEB became what it is today. La Poutine Week donates a portion of poutine sales to ANEB to help Quebecers with eating disorders.

Vote! Vote! Vote!Taste and Vote.

Every year, between February 1-7, restaurants from different cities across Canada make special poutines just for La Poutine Week. Poutine-lovers all over get a chance to discover these delicious creations and vote for their favorite ones.
